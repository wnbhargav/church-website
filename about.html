About Our Church

Founded in 1990, Grace Community Church is a welcoming place of worship where we gather to grow in faith, love, and service. Our mission is to share the Gospel, support our community, and strengthen families through the love of Christ.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the power of God’s Word, the importance of prayer, and living as disciples of Jesus Christ. We follow biblical principles and seek to serve our neighbors with compassion.

Our Leadership

Our pastors, elders, and volunteers work together to guide our congregation and organize ministries that support youth, families, and the community.
